Barn Buddies

Adopting a barn cat from the Bowling Green Humane Society can help save the life of a cat who may not have other options.
There is NO ADOPTION FEE for barn cats!
If you can offer a cat shelter in your barn, stable, or other outbuilding away from high-traffic streets:
please contact us at the adoption office to submit an application and we will contact you with our next available barn buddy!
Cats selected by our staff for the Barn Buddies program may be FERAL or SEMI-FERAL.
They will be cats who are unaccustomed to living indoors and/or who are unsocial with humans and prefer the company of other cats.

What We Provide for Barn Cats:
  • Spay or Neuter
  • Vaccinations (FVRCP and Rabies)
  • Microchip (permanent identification)
What You Provide for Barn Cats:
  • Shelter in barn, stable, or other outbuilding
  • Daily food and water
  • Long term Veterinary care, as needed
  • A secure place to keep the cat for the first 3-5 weeks, while the cat acclimates to your barn.
    This can be a tack room or an indoor enclosure that they cannot escape from.
    In some cases we may be able to lend you a crate or cage for the introductory period.
