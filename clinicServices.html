Clinic & Services

Spay/Neuter Clinic

The Hotaling Spay/Neuter clinic opened its doors in June of 2013. It is a tool in combating our community's overpopulation, and our goal is to provide a safe and affordable place for people to bring their pets to have them sterilized. Please help to support our efforts.

Pet Licensing

Dog and Cat Licensing The City of Bowling Green requires annual licensing of all dogs and cats three (3) months of age or older.

Microchipping

A microchip is a small, electronic chip enclosed in a glass cylinder that is about the same size as a grain of rice. The microchip itself does not have a batteryâ€”it is activated by a scanner that is passed over the area, and the radiowaves put out by the scanner activate the chip. The chip transmits the identification number to the scanner, which displays the number on the screen. The microchip itself is also called a transponder.
